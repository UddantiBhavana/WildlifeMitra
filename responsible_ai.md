# Responsible AI Statement - WildlifeMitra

## Transparency
This application uses **Agentic RAG** (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) over official government and biodiversity documents. All responses are generated by Large Language Models (Groq) and should be treated as **guidance only**.

## Disclaimers
- This tool is **not a substitute** for official advice from the Andhra Pradesh Forest Department.
- Always contact local authorities or the State Wildlife Helpline (**1926**) in case of emergencies.
- AI responses may contain inaccuracies — please verify critical information.

## Privacy & Data Handling
- No personal or sensitive user data is stored.
- Conversations are not saved after the session ends.
- All processing happens locally or through secure API calls.

## Ethical Considerations
- Designed to support both **human safety** and **wildlife welfare**.
- Promotes coexistence rather than conflict.
- Focuses on empowering rural communities with accessible information.

## Limitations
- Telugu responses may vary in quality.
- Performance depends on the quality and coverage of uploaded documents.
- Not intended for real-time crisis management without human verification.
